Centific Redmond HQ Tour (12/8/23)

The BC Computer Programming Club (CPC), in collaboration with the BC Tech Startup Club (TSC), delved into Centific's AI and innovation hub, gaining firsthand insights into groundbreaking work. Beyond classroom boundaries, this visit bridged theory with practical applications, fueling a passion for tech's potential. Witnessing AI in action at Centific's lab left an indelible mark, igniting curiosity to explore further. Enormous thanks to the Centific team, especially Jess Zhu, Preti Singh, SHRM-CP, Joe Thomas, Brian Scroggs, Meher Dinesh Naroju, and Jagadish Garimella, for an enriching and memorable experience, showcasing thrilling tech possibilities.

UW Seattle CS Guest Speakers (12/1/23)

Bellevue College Computer Programming Club (CPC), in collaboration with Tech Startup Club (TSC) and Tech Club (TC), hosted an event at the N201 building on December 1, 2023, featuring UW’s Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering alumni: Max (Founder of this club! and BC alumni), Jintae, and Berky. Despite an attendance surge beyond 50 causing a food shortage, the event smoothly transitioned from networking to insightful presentations. Max discussed UW's academics, Jintae covered transfer challenges for international students, and Berky offered a comprehensive guide on applications and transitions, benefiting aspiring UW Computer Science students.

Microsoft Redmond HQ Tour (11/10/23)

On Veterans Day 2023, a momentous occasion unfolded as the BC Computer Programming Club, joined by the BC Tech Club and BC Tech Startup Club, embarked on an insightful tour of the renowned Microsoft HQ in Redmond, WA. Welcoming approximately 50 attendees, this excursion provided a firsthand glimpse into one of the most iconic landmarks within the tech industry. It was an enriching experience, delving into the heart of innovation and technology that defines Microsoft's legacy.
