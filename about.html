Weekly Meetings

We have weekly meetings during regular academic quarters on Fridays from 2-3pm in building U208.

Our weekly meetings usually start out with a presentation from Club Officers about future club events and any other club news, then people will work on club coding projects and just hang out!

Mission Statement

Our mission at the Bellevue College Computer Programming Club is to create a supportive and collaborative community where students of all skill levels can come together to explore, learn, and excel in the field of computer programming. We are dedicated to providing opportunities for skill development, networking, and personal growth. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and engaging events, we aim to empower our members to become innovative and confident programmers who can make a positive impact in the tech industry.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading force in fostering a culture of innovation and excellence in computer programming at Bellevue College. We aspire to equip our members with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to become industry leaders and problem solvers. We envision a future where the Bellevue College Computer Programming Club is recognized for producing top-notch programmers who drive technological advancements and contribute to the growth of our local and global communities.
