UW Seattle CS Guest Speakers (12/1/23)

Bellevue College Computer Programming Club (CPC), in collaboration with Tech Startup Club (TSC) and Tech Club (TC), hosted an event at the N201 building on December 1, 2023, featuring UW’s Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering alumni: Max (Founder of this club! and BC alumni), Jintae, and Berky. Despite an attendance surge beyond 50 causing a food shortage, the event smoothly transitioned from networking to insightful presentations. Max discussed UW's academics, Jintae covered transfer challenges for international students, and Berky offered a comprehensive guide on applications and transitions, benefiting aspiring UW Computer Science students.
